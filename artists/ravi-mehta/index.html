About Ravi

Delhi's most trusted tattoo artist with over 15 years of professional experience

Professional Journey

Ravi Mehta started his tattoo journey in 2009 and has since become one of Delhi's most sought-after tattoo artists. With over 5,000+ successful tattoos and countless satisfied clients, Ravi specializes in bringing your vision to life with precision and artistry.

Known for his meticulous attention to detail and ability to work across multiple styles, Ravi has built a reputation for creating custom designs that tell unique stories. His work has been featured in various tattoo magazines and art exhibitions across Delhi NCR.

Philosophy & Approach

"Every tattoo is a personal story waiting to be told. My job is to listen, understand, and translate that story into art that will last a lifetime." - Ravi Mehta

Ravi believes in collaborative design, working closely with each client to ensure their tattoo perfectly represents their vision. His studio maintains the highest standards of hygiene and safety, using only premium equipment and FDA-approved inks.
