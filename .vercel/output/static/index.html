Complete Tattoo Aftercare & Healing Guide

Proper aftercare ensures your tattoo heals perfectly and looks amazing for years. Follow our proven process for optimal results.

Your Tattoo Healing Journey

1-3

Days 1-3

Initial healing, some bleeding & plasma discharge. Keep covered, gentle cleaning.

4-14

Days 4-14

Peeling & scabbing phase. DO NOT pick or scratch. Keep moisturized.

15-30

Days 15-30

Deep healing continues. Colors settle, skin regenerates. Continue care routine.

2-3M

Months 2-3

Complete healing. Tattoo reaches final appearance. Perfect time for touch-ups if needed.

First 48 Hours

  • ✓ Keep bandage on for 2-4 hours (per artist instruction)
  • ✓ Gently wash with lukewarm water & antibacterial soap
  • ✓ Pat dry with clean paper towel (not cloth)
  • ✓ Apply thin layer of provided aftercare balm
  • ✓ Avoid tight clothing over tattoo area
  • ✓ Sleep on clean sheets, avoid laying on tattoo

Week 1-2 Routine

  • ✓ Wash 2-3 times daily with gentle antibacterial soap
  • ✓ Apply aftercare lotion when skin feels tight
  • ✓ Quick showers only (no soaking/swimming)
  • ✓ Wear loose, breathable clothing
  • ✓ Avoid direct sunlight and tanning
  • ✓ DO NOT pick at scabs or peeling skin

Lifetime Protection

  • ✓ Always use SPF 30+ sunscreen on healed tattoo
  • ✓ Moisturize regularly to keep skin healthy
  • ✓ Stay hydrated for better skin elasticity
  • ✓ Avoid excessive weight gain/loss in tattooed area
  • ✓ Schedule touch-ups every 5-10 years if needed
  • ✓ Contact us immediately if signs of infection

⚠️ Contact Us Immediately If You Experience:

  • • Excessive swelling beyond day 3
  • • Pus or unusual discharge
  • • Red streaking from tattoo area
  • • Fever or chills
  • • Unusual pain that worsens
  • • Allergic reaction symptoms
