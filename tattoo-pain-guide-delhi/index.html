💪 Pain Management Secrets (From 10+ Years in Delhi)

🌟 BEFORE Your Appointment

  • Eat Well: Have a proper meal 2 hours before. Low blood sugar = more pain
  • Sleep Properly: Like, actually get 7-8 hours. Tired body feels more pain
  • Hydrate: Drink lots of water day before. Delhi heat dehydrates you anyway
  • Avoid Alcohol: No drinking 24 hours before. It thins blood and makes you bleed more
  • No Painkillers: Don't take aspirin or ibuprofen - they increase bleeding

⚡ DURING Your Session

  • Breathe: Deep breathing actually helps. I tell all my clients this
  • Talk to Us: Distract yourself. We love chatting about Delhi, Bollywood, whatever
  • Take Breaks: Don't be a hero. We pause whenever you need it
  • Bring Music/Earphones: Your favorite playlist makes time fly
  • Squeeze Something: Stress ball or just make fists. Helps release tension

💊 What About Numbing Cream? (Honest Opinion)

Everyone asks about numbing cream, and you know what? I'm gonna give you the real deal here.

The Good: Yeah, it works for about 1-2 hours. Can help with initial anxiety.

The Bad: It wears off during longer sessions. Sometimes makes skin swell. Can affect ink absorption.

My Take: For small tattoos (under 2 hours), go for it if you're really worried. For big pieces? You're gonna have to deal with pain anyway, so might as well get used to it.
