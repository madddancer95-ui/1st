HOW TO TAKE CARE OF YOUR NEW TATTOO

Look, we make amazing tattoos but you gotta take care of them properly. Best tattoo studio in Delhi NCR can't help if you mess up the healing. Follow this guide and your tattoo will look perfect forever.

✓

First Day - Don't Panic

Leave that protective film on for few hours. Then wash gently with normal soap and warm water. Pat dry, don't rub.

✓

Keep It Clean Daily

Wash it 2-3 times every day. Use simple soap, nothing fancy. Put a tiny bit of healing cream. Don't go crazy with cream.

✓

Don't Do These Things

No swimming pools or gym saunas for 2-3 weeks. Stay away from direct sun. And seriously, don't pick at the peeling skin.

✓

What Normal Healing Looks Like

Little bit red and puffy first few days - totally normal. Starts peeling around day 4-7. Takes 2-4 weeks to fully heal.

✓

Keep It Looking Good Forever

Use sunscreen on your tattoo when going out. Keep it moisturized with good lotion. Come back for free touch ups if needed.
